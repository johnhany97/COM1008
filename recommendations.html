    Recommendations

    On this page, you will find our recommendations to our friend websites offering you with more puzzles than those you'd find here. Our friend websites have different types of puzzles compared to the ones we have here in Puzzle Mania. We believe that in order to help our audience, we should be providing you with all the proper sources of great puzzles to help you have fun and improve your solving skills. So, we did all the hardwork for you and provide you here with a list of the best puzzles you can find on the internet (Other than Puzzle Mania of course)

    Jigsaw Explorer

    Jigsaw Explorer is quite an interesting website. They offer tons of jigsaw puzzles that emulate real life games and are quite fascinating and fun to play with. The variety of photos offered too is quite enormous which is splendid!

    MiniClip

    MiniClip is the most famous games website on the internet. They have games of all types, shapes and sizes! However, their puzzle collection is quite interesting. They have a couple of puzzle games that are quite unique and fun to play with. We definitely recommend you take a look there.

    Pzzls

    Pzzls is a puzzles website of a different type. They offer lots of different logical puzzles, riddles, scientific and mathematical puzzles. They are not the easiest to solve but they're the most enjoyable when solved! Definitely recommend this website if you want to take your puzzle solving to the next level.
